Welcome

notifyr is a Firefox add—on which notifies you of new mail via a toast-like notification.

Installation

  1. Download notifyr.xpi and drag-and-drop it into your Firefox window.
  2. Follow the on-screen instructions to install notifyr.

Usage

  1. Keep your email open in a Firefox window.
  2. You will be notified of new mail via a toast—like notification (similar to the one in the screenshots below).

To configure notifyr, visit the add—on preferences page from Tools » Add—ons » Extensions » notifyr » Preferences.

notifyr currently supports English (en—US) and German (de—DE) locales.
